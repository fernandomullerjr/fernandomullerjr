### Hello everyone 👋

I am Fernando Müller, SRE/DevOps Engineering Manager at [Appmax](https://www.appmax.com.br/), leading the team that keeps our payment infrastructure reliable and scalable.

I have a passion for learning and sharing my knowledge with others as publicly as possible.
Focused on AWS, Kubernetes, Terraform, Ansible and Linux, automating with Python, Go and Shell. On the observability and CI/CD side, I work with Prometheus, Grafana, Elastic, ArgoCD and Bitbucket Pipelines.

I'm the founder of the blog <a href="https://devopsmind.com.br">devopsmind.com.br</a>, where I post tips related to the DevOps world.
